Carbonara recipe

Back

Description

Carbonara is a quick and simple pasta dish mainly consisting of meat, cheese, eggs & pasta.

Ingredients

  • Parmiggiano Reggiano
  • Egg yolks
  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Finely chopped parseley

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta until 1 minute before Al Dente
  2. Add in your pancetta to a pan and render the fat
  3. Take out the pasta 1 minute before Al Dente and add in to the pan with the pancetta
  4. Add in pasta water as needed
  5. Grate parmiggiano reggiano and mix with egg yolks until consistent
  6. Add pasta and pancetta to egg yolk mixture and mix
  7. Lastly, grate more parmiggiano over the pasta and garnish with parsley and serve
