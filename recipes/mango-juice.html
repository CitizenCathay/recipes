Mango juice recipe

Back

Description

Mango juice is very refreshing and one of mu favourite drinks.

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • Ice
  • Suagr syrup

Steps

  1. Dice the mangoes into cubes.
  2. Add ice to a blender.
  3. Add one tablespoon of sugar syrup into blender.
  4. Add mangoes into the blender.
  5. Garnish with a leaf of parsley.
